# lambada 2.3.0

## `humane` bumped to v0.2.0: future-time wording changed

`timeAgo` now renders future timestamps as `"in 3 minutes"` instead of
`"3 minutes from now"`, matching Swift's `RelativeDateTimeFormatter`
exactly (see [`humane` v0.2.0](https://github.com/woodie/humane/blob/main/docs/releases/v0.2.0.md)).
Past-side wording (`"3 minutes ago"`) and the collapsed sub-minute bucket's
past side (`"less than a minute ago"`) are unchanged; the collapsed
sub-minute future side is now `"in less than a minute"`.

No other user-facing changes.
